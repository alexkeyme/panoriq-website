03 — Capabilities

Four layers, one panorama.

Each capability mirrors a layer of the mark — from deep context to the surface signals you rely on day to day.

Unified context

Pull everything that matters — product signals, ops data, customer feedback — into a single source of context that stays current on its own.

  • — Bi-directional sync with the tools you already use
  • — Zero-config schema detection
Ⅱ — Mid

Live pattern discovery

Spot what changed, what's working, and what needs attention — without writing another query. Panoriq continuously scans for deviations that matter.
