How to read your result - total / 24
0-8
The “seems to work” zone. Most teams are here. The risk is that you only find out what’s broken when a user does. Start with dimensions 1, 2, and 4.
9-16
Some structure, not yet a system. Typical gap: you have a dataset and traces, but no automated runs and no production feedback loop. Connect what you already have.
17-24
A real evaluation discipline. The remaining work is depth - better metrics, harder edge cases, faster iteration. The structure is in place.
A note on dimension 1

This is the one most teams skip and most regret skipping. “What does good look like?” is not an engineering question - it requires product, domain experts, and the people who will actually use the output. If you can’t answer it, no amount of tooling setup will help.
