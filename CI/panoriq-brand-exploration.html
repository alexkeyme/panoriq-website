Recommendations

  • Wordmark: Move to Manrope (option 2) or the accented 'q' variant (option 8). Both solve the "generic sans" problem and tie the mark to the illustration's modern feel.
  • Mark: Thin the outer circle stroke by ~30%, and commission a simplified favicon-scale version (single color, flattened whale silhouette inside circle).
  • Warm accent: Pick Coral (#FF7A59) as your primary warm — it complements cyan without competing. Use sparingly: CTAs, highlights, "new" chips.
  • Grays: Adopt the 9-step scale above. You'll need all of it for any real UI work.
  • Contrast: Don't use Pale Aqua for text — it only passes WCAG on navy/black. For text on light backgrounds, default to Navy or Gray 600+.
  • Teal shift: The revised Teal (#1F6B6B) sits further from Cyan in both hue and value, so charts and categorical visualizations won't muddy.
